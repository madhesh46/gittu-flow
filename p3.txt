  <h1>Discovering Tiruchirappalli</h1>
  <p>Trichy, a lively city in Tamil Nadu, is rich in history and culture. It’s known for ancient temples, busy markets, and a strong regional presence.</p>

  <h2>Rockfort Temple</h2>
  <p>The famous Rockfort Ucchi Pillayar Temple sits atop a giant rock, offering stunning city views after a steep climb.</p>

  <h3>Other Attractions</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Mallakottai – Popular local area</li>
    <li>Cauvery River – Well-known and scenic</li>
    <li>Butterfly Park – Great for nature lovers</li>
  </ul>

  <p>Trichy's markets like Teppakulam are colorful and full of life. Try local foods like dosa and idli for a true South Indian taste!</p>
